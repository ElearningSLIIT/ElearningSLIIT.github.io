eLearning Best Practices


The Computing Faculty of Sri Lankan Institute of Information Technology started E-learning research group on 22nd April 2020 to promote research, to improve and apply teaching best practices using research and to collaborate with other local and international institutes. Our passion is for researching about new teaching concepts, frameworks, tools and to improve methodologies used in teaching and make positive difference among academia. Specially, make the society familiar with concepts, tools and frameworks related to e-learning.
