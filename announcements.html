"MoocRec- MOOCs Recommender Based Learning styles" Won the Digital Social Impact award in e-Swabhimani, 2018

"MoocRec- MOOCs Recommender Based Learning styles" Won the Digital Social Impact award in e-Swabhimani awards in year 2018,under the category of Learning and Education. This was a collaborative achievement of Saugat Aryal, Anjana Shriwantha Porawagama, Makulle GedaraSajeeva Hasith,Sachini Chole Thoradeniya, who were undergraduates at SLIIT. The research project was supervised by Dr.Nuwan Kodagoda and co-supervised by Ms.Kushnara Suriyawansa.
